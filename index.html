Movies Today

Movies are wonderful. They bring people together and entertain billions of people all around the world. The main place where movies are made is inside of Hollywood. Hollywood is a magical place where people come together to be able to create amazing content that brings everyone together. Recently, other studios that used to just do streaming have entered the content creation sphere. The main competitors in this are Netflix, Hulu, and Amazon Studios. These studios are making incredibly high budget content without the same bounds that Hollywood has. They are able to deliver their content right to their users via their streaming services.

Today, we have many different genres of movies. These movies allow everyone to pick exactly what they want to watch. It allows for people to be able to only watch what they enjoy and what entertains them. There are also movies that span genres. This means that people can come together through these shared genres. Every year, more and more movies are created. This allows for more and more ideas to be put out there. Many people believe that film is an art. As time goes on, however, much of this art is simply used for entertainment and nothing more.
